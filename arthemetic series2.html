Arithmetic Sum Problems

Problem 1: Sum of the First n Terms

Given an arithmetic sequence with the first term \( a_1 = 5 \) and a common difference \( d = 3 \), find the sum of the first 20 terms.

Problem 2: Finding the Sum of a Range of Terms

In an arithmetic sequence, the first term is \( a_1 = 7 \) and the common difference is \( d = 4 \). Calculate the sum of terms from the 10th to the 20th term.

Problem 3: Sum Using the Last Term

The first term of an arithmetic sequence is \( a_1 = 2 \), and the last term is \( a_n = 50 \). If the number of terms is 25, find the sum of the sequence.

Problem 4: Total Sum Given Partial Sums

If the sum of the first 15 terms of an arithmetic sequence is 120 and the sum of the first 30 terms is 465, find the common difference \( d \).

Problem 5: Sum of Even-Indexed Terms

Given an arithmetic sequence with the first term \( a_1 = 3 \) and common difference \( d = 5 \), calculate the sum of the even-indexed terms from \( a_2 \) to \( a_{20} \).

Problem 6: Arithmetic Sum of Consecutive Integers

Find the sum of all integers from 1 to 100. Treat the sequence as an arithmetic sequence where the first term is 1 and the common difference is 1.

Problem 7: Sum of a Specific Number of Terms

If the sum of the first 12 terms of an arithmetic sequence is 156, and the first term is 4, find the common difference \( d \).

Problem 8: Finding the Number of Terms Given a Sum

In an arithmetic sequence, the first term is \( a_1 = 6 \) and the common difference is \( d = 2 \). If the sum of the sequence is 120, find the number of terms.

Problem 9: Sum of Alternating Terms

Given an arithmetic sequence where the first term is \( a_1 = 10 \) and the common difference is \( d = 3 \), find the sum of every alternate term from the 1st to the 15th term (i.e., sum of terms \( a_1, a_3, a_5, \dots \)).

Problem 10: Sum of Terms in a Range with a Given Last Term

If the last term of an arithmetic sequence is 72, the first term is 12, and the common difference is 6, find the sum of all terms in the sequence.
